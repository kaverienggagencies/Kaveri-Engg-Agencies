Simple Buying Guide

Which pump should you choose?

Choosing the correct motor depends mainly on water usage. For less usage, single phase motors are preferred. For more usage and heavy-duty water needs, three phase borewell pump sets are preferred.

Single Phase Water Motor

Recommended for houses, homes, small shops, and small industries where water usage is comparatively less.

Three Phase Borewell Pump Set

Recommended for agriculture borewells, shallow wells, big shopping malls, and large industrial areas where water usage is high.
